ATTENTION NEW MEMBERS
If you are mailing in your YCAS Membership application, please make sure it includes your email address so we may contact you, confirm your information, and invite you to our email group.

If you are paying via PayPal, please make sure you click this link to send us an email once you complete your PayPal transaction. This will collect the information we need to process your application, contact you, and invite you to our email group.

Choose Membership

Choose your membership type and Add to Cart. Adding an item will open a new window within Pay Pal. Click this link to send your membership details via email.

Check your totals

Cost is only $20 (or less) for Single membership or $30 (or less) for Single Family household. Memberships are Pro-Rated based on the month you join. Please add only one of the Membership options below to your Pay Pal shopping cart.

Benefits:

Access to the Observatory and telescopes.
Free Planetarium shows to YCAS members.
Membership in The Astronomical League, The benefits of ALCOR , a national association of astronomy clubs.
4 quarterly issues of The Astronomical League’s newsletter, “Reflector”
Free to YCAS Members YCAS Astronomy classes.
Discounts on books and various other publications through The Astronomical League.
Use of the club’s library of technical and reference books, slides, and videos.
Use of several of the club’s 8 inch Dobsonian telescopes and Solar telescope which are available for sign out use.
Members Dark Sky Trips.
Adult members (over 21) can vote on all Society concerns and are eligible to be elected as a Society officers. Student membership excluded.
Learn from some of the areas most experienced observers.
