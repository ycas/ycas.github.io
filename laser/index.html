Private Show Reservation Information

The planetarium seats 60 people. Due to the nature of the shows, we recommend a maximum of 36 people per show. Note: Laser shows use flashing lights and rapidly changing images, which may affect photosensitive viewers. Viewer discretion is advised. Filming and live streaming laser shows is prohibited.

Cost is $250 for a private show, which includes a 55-minute laser show and a 10-minute Q&A session with the laser operator.

Laser shows are available for a limited time, so please reserve your private show as soon as possible. Private programs can be reserved on our Cur8.com page.
Click here to reserve a private show.

You do not need to print the tickets, just present photo identification.

If you have any questions, please contact us at laser@astroyork.com
