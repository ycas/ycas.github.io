Join us for Astronomy 101, a public lecture series featuring engaging talks by experienced speakers covering the fundamentals of astronomy and modern space science. Each session is designed for general audiences, with time built in for questions and discussion.

**Who Should Attend?** Beginners with no prior astronomy background, Amateur
astronomers and space enthusiasts, families, students, and lifelong learners. Come
explore the universe—one Saturday night at a time.

**Event Details:**<br>
* starting May 9, 2026 @6pm <br>
* class at John Rudy Park Observatory<br>
* free for members<br>
* $30 for non-members<br>
